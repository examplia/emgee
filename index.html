Disclaimer

  • You are able to load in any map you want in game. As long as Roblox Community Standards are followed, you will not be banned for uploading a map.
  • With that being said, uploading maps with any form of inappropriate imagery, language, or innuendo, even if it is hidden, WILL RESULT IN A PERMANENT BAN.
    • Just be reasonable, and ask yourself: “would a moderator approve of your map if they were to inspect it?”
    • Stick to the prop pack if you wish to use text for gameplay on your map.

TC2 Workshop Map Submission Criteria

  • The current TC2 map pool contains many TF2 maps, and that is something we are looking to change.
  • We want future maps to be high quality, completely original and consistent with other maps.
    • The only map that currently meets these standards is 3cp_yellowvalley, and even it has some flaws.
      • To increase your chances of your map being added, study 3cp_yellowvalley very attentively: attention to detail, prop frequency, terrain creation, etc.
      • Maps should not look like they are part of 3cp_yellowvalley, but rather part of the same world.
    • High quality means craftsmanship—when you look closer, you say “a lot of effort was put into this.”
    • Completely original means every aspect was created by the mapper. Instead of copying TF2, ask yourself “what would work here?”
    • Consistent means meeting the same detail level and art-style as other maps.
      • Although 3cp_yellowvalley is the only satisfactory map now, future maps should match its art-style, not TF2’s.
  • More resources on map detailing/layouts are coming, but for now:
    • Do not use any TF2 props—use the provided prop pack and get creative.
    • Focus on parts for detailing, not props. Make walls pretty!
    • Master triangle terrain: study 3cp_yellowvalley and see how little props are needed outdoors.
    • For similarly themed areas, copy 3cp_yellowvalley designs 1:1 for consistency.
    • Art-style is 2000–2010: nature reserves, mines, factories—GRN protects, RED exploits.
  • For any questions regarding your maps, ping @FWEEEEEE on the official TC2 Discord!
